"# Notion\n\nThis guide walks you through setting up the Notion integration within Boltic.\n\n## Prerequisites\n\nTo set up the Notion integration, you'll need:\n\n- A Notion account\n\nIf you don't have a Notion account, you can create one at <a href=\"https://www.notion.so/signup\" target=\"_blank\">Notion Sign Up</a>.\n\n## Setting Up the Notion Integration in Boltic\n\n1. **Log In**\n\n   - Log into your Notion account.\n\n2. **Log into Boltic**\n\n   - Log into your Boltic account or navigate to the Boltic Open Source dashboard.\n\n3. **Navigate to Integrations**\n\n   - Click on `Sources` and then click on `+ Add New Source`.\n\n4. **Select Source Type**\n\n   - On the Set up the source page, search for `Notion` from the search bar and select it.\n\n5. **Authenticate Notion Account**\n\n   - Click `Connect to Notion`.\n   - You will be redirected to the Notion website.\n   - If you are already logged into Notion, the connection will be automatically established. If not, log into your Notion account.\n\n6. **Configure Integration**\n\n   - **Name:** Enter a name for the Notion integration.\n   - **Workspace:** Select the workspace you want to connect.\n\n7. **Details (Optional)**\n\n   - **Tags:** Add any tags for categorization.\n   - **Description:** Provide a brief description of the integration.\n\n8. **Test and Save Integration**\n\n   - Click `Test Connection` to ensure the connection is successful.\n   - Once the connection test is successful, click `Set up source` to complete the configuration.\n\n## Support\n\nIf you need further assistance, please contact Boltic support or visit our <a href=\"https://docs.boltic.io\" target=\"_blank\">Support Documentation</a>.\n"
