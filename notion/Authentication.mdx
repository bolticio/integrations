# Notion

This guide walks you through setting up the Notion integration within Boltic.

## Prerequisites

To set up the Notion integration, you'll need:

- A Notion account

If you don't have a Notion account, you can create one at <a href="https://www.notion.so/signup" target="_blank">Notion Sign Up</a>.

## Setting Up the Notion Integration in Boltic

1. **Log In**

   - Log into your Notion account.

2. **Log into Boltic**

   - Log into your Boltic account or navigate to the Boltic Open Source dashboard.

3. **Navigate to Integrations**

   - Click on `Sources` and then click on `+ Add New Source`.

4. **Select Source Type**

   - On the Set up the source page, search for `Notion` from the search bar and select it.

5. **Authenticate Notion Account**

   - Click `Connect to Notion`.
   - You will be redirected to the Notion website.
   - If you are already logged into Notion, the connection will be automatically established. If not, log into your Notion account.

6. **Configure Integration**

   - **Name:** Enter a name for the Notion integration.
   - **Workspace:** Select the workspace you want to connect.

7. **Details (Optional)**

   - **Tags:** Add any tags for categorization.
   - **Description:** Provide a brief description of the integration.

8. **Test and Save Integration**

   - Click `Test Connection` to ensure the connection is successful.
   - Once the connection test is successful, click `Set up source` to complete the configuration.
