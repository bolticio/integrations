"# Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) Integration\n\nThis guide walks you through setting up the Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) integration within Boltic.\n\n## Prerequisites\n\nTo retrieve client credentials, you'll need:\n\n-   A registered <a href=\"https://platform.fynd.com/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>Fynd Commerce account</span></a> with access to at least one company.\n\n## Setting Up the Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) Integration in Boltic\n\n1. **Log In**\n\n    - Log into your <a href=\"https://platform.fynd.com/\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>Fynd Commerce account</span></a>.\n\n2. **Select Company**\n\n    - If you have access to multiple companies, you will be prompted to select one. If not, the system will redirect you to the \"Create Company\" form.\n\n3. **Access Fynd Commerce Dashboard**\n\n    - Once you have selected the company, you will be directed to the Fynd Commerce Dashboard.\n\n4. **Navigate to Developers Tab**\n\n    - Click on the `Developers` tab in the sidebar. This action will open the `APIs & SDKs` page.\n\n5. **Access Clients Tab**\n\n    - Click on the `Clients` tab to view a list of existing clients.\n\n6. **Create Client or use existing ones**\n\n    - Click on `Create Client` and fill in the necessary details and permissions to create a new client.\n\n    - You can also use any already exisiting credential.\n\n7. **Retrieve Credentials**\n\n    - After creating the client, you will be able to view your `Client ID` and `Client Secret`.\n\n8. **Log into Boltic**\n\n    - Log into your Boltic account or navigate to the Boltic Open Source dashboard.\n\n9. **Navigate to Integrations**\n\n    - Click on `Sources` and then click on `+ Add New source`.\n\n10. **Select Source Type**\n\n    - On the Set up the source page, search for `Fynd Commerce` from the search bar and select it.\n\n11. **Configure Integration**\n\n    - **Name:** Enter a name for the Fynd Commerce integration.\n\n    - **Company ID:** Enter the Company ID from the Fynd Commerce.\n\n    - **Client ID:** Enter the Client ID obtained from the Fynd Commerce.\n\n    - **Client Secret:** Enter the Client Secret obtained from the Fynd Commerce.\n\n12. **Details (Optional)**\n\n    - **Tags:** Add any tags for categorization.\n\n    - **Description:** Provide a brief description of the integration.\n\n13. **Test and Save Integration**\n\n    - Click `Test & Save` to ensure the connection is successful.\n\n    - Once the connection test is successful, your integration will be saved.\n\n## Support\n\nFor further assistance, please contact Fynd Commerce support or refer to our <a href=\"https://platform.fynd.com/help\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>Support Documentation</span></a>.\n"
