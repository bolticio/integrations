# Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) Integration

This guide walks you through setting up the Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) integration within Boltic.

## Prerequisites

To retrieve client credentials, you'll need:

-   A registered <a href="https://platform.fynd.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span>Fynd Commerce account</span></a> with access to at least one company.

## Setting Up the Fynd Commerce ( Platform ) Integration in Boltic

1. **Log In**

    - Log into your <a href="https://platform.fynd.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener"><span>Fynd Commerce account</span></a>.

2. **Select Company**

    - If you have access to multiple companies, you will be prompted to select one. If not, the system will redirect you to the "Create Company" form.

3. **Access Fynd Commerce Dashboard**

    - Once you have selected the company, you will be directed to the Fynd Commerce Dashboard.

4. **Navigate to Developers Tab**

    - Click on the `Developers` tab in the sidebar. This action will open the `APIs & SDKs` page.

5. **Access Clients Tab**

    - Click on the `Clients` tab to view a list of existing clients.

6. **Create Client or use existing ones**

    - Click on `Create Client` and fill in the necessary details and permissions to create a new client.

    - You can also use any already exisiting credential.

7. **Retrieve Credentials**

    - After creating the client, you will be able to view your `Client ID` and `Client Secret`.

8. **Log into Boltic**

    - Log into your Boltic account or navigate to the Boltic Open Source dashboard.

9. **Navigate to Integrations**

    - Click on `Sources` and then click on `+ Add New source`.

10. **Select Source Type**

    - On the Set up the source page, search for `Fynd Commerce` from the search bar and select it.

11. **Configure Integration**

    - **Name:** Enter a name for the Fynd Commerce integration.

    - **Company ID:** Enter the Company ID from the Fynd Commerce.

    - **Client ID:** Enter the Client ID obtained from the Fynd Commerce.

    - **Client Secret:** Enter the Client Secret obtained from the Fynd Commerce.

12. **Details (Optional)**

    - **Tags:** Add any tags for categorization.

    - **Description:** Provide a brief description of the integration.

13. **Test and Save Integration**

    - Click `Test & Save` to ensure the connection is successful.

    - Once the connection test is successful, your integration will be saved.
