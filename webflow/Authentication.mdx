"\n\n# Webflow\n\nThis guide walks you through setting up the Webflow integration within Boltic.\n\n\n\n## Prerequisites\n\nTo set up the Webflow integration, you'll need:\n\n- A Webflow account  \n  You can create an account at <a href=\"https://webflow.com\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>Webflow</span></a>.\n\n\n\n## Setting Up the Webflow Integration in Boltic\n\n1. **Log In**\n\n   - Log into your Webflow account.\n\n\n\n2. **Log into Boltic**\n\n   - Log into your Boltic account or navigate to the Boltic Open Source dashboard.\n\n\n\n3. **Navigate to Integrations**\n\n   - Click on `Sources` and then click on `+ Add New source`.\n\n\n\n4. **Select Source Type**\n\n   - On the Set up the source page, search for `Webflow` from the search bar and select it.\n\n\n\n5. **Create a Connection**\n\n   - Click `Create a Connection`.\n\n   - Optional: In the **Connection name** field, enter a name for the connection.\n\n   - Optional: Click `Show Advanced Settings` and enter your custom app's client credentials. To create a custom app, refer to the [Webflow OAuth2.0: App Registration documentation](https://university.webflow.com/lesson/oauth-2-0-authentication).\n\n     - Note: If requested, use the following redirect URI: <a href=\"https://www.integromat.com/oauth/cb/webflow2\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>https://www.integromat.com/oauth/cb/webflow2</span></a>.\n\n\n\n6. **Authenticate Webflow Account**\n\n   - Click `Save`.\n\n   - If prompted, log in to your Webflow account and confirm the access.\n\n\n\n7. **Details (Optional)**\n\n   - **Tags:** Add any tags for categorization.\n\n   - **Description:** Provide a brief description of the integration.\n\n\n\n8. **Test and Save Integration**\n\n   - Click `Test Connection` to ensure the connection is successful.\n\n   - Once the connection test is successful, click `Set up source` to complete the configuration.\n\n\n\n## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)\n\n1.  **How do I find my Webflow API token?**\n\n- To obtain your Webflow API token, navigate to your Webflow project settings and look for the API tokens section. For detailed instructions, refer to Webflow's official documentation on [API tokens](https://university.webflow.com/lesson/api-tokens).\n\n\n\n2.  **What should I do if my connection fails?**\n\n- Ensure your API token is correct and valid.\n\n- Verify that the client ID and client secret (if using a custom app) are accurate.\n\n- Double-check the domain URL for any typos.\n\n- Ensure your Webflow account has the necessary permissions.\n\n- Check your network connection and firewall settings for potential issues.\n\n\n\n3.  **How often is the data synced?**\n\n\n- The data sync frequency depends on the settings you configure during the setup process. You can customize the sync frequency based on your needs.\n\n\n\n4.  **Can I customize the data being synced?**\n\n- Yes, you can customize the data being synced by selecting specific modules and configuring the relevant settings during the setup process.\n\n\n\n## Support\n\nIf you need further assistance, please contact Boltic support or visit our <a href=\"https://docs.boltic.io\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>Support Documentation</span></a>.\n\n\n\n"
