"# HubSpot Authentication\n\n## Overview\n\nHubSpot's free CRM powers your customer support, sales, and marketing with easy-to-use features like live chat, meeting scheduling, and email tracking.\n\n## Authentication Ways\n\n-   Private App Access Token\n\n## Private App Access Token\n\nTo authenticate with HubSpot using a Private App, you need to create a Private App and generate an Access Token.\n\n**Steps:**\n1. Log in to your HubSpot account.\n2. Go to **Settings** > **Integrations** > **Private Apps**.\n3. Click **Create a private app**.\n4. Enter a name and description for your app.\n5. **Add the following scopes to your Private App:**\n\n    ```\n    crm.objects.subscriptions.write\n    crm.objects.line_items.read\n    crm.objects.line_items.write\n    crm.schemas.line_items.read\n    forms\n    tickets\n    communication_preferences.read\n    crm.lists.read\n    crm.objects.contacts.read\n    crm.objects.subscriptions.read\n    crm.schemas.subscriptions.read\n    crm.schemas.commercepayments.read\n    crm.objects.commercepayments.read \n    crm.objects.contacts.write\n    crm.objects.users.write\n    crm.objects.marketing_events.read\n    crm.objects.marketing_events.write\n    crm.objects.companies.write\n    crm.schemas.contacts.read\n    crm.pipelines.orders.write\n    crm.pipelines.orders.read\n    crm.objects.leads.read\n    crm.objects.leads.write\n    crm.lists.write\n    crm.objects.companies.read\n    crm.objects.deals.read\n    crm.objects.deals.write\n    crm.schemas.companies.read\n    crm.schemas.companies.write\n    crm.schemas.contacts.write\n    crm.schemas.deals.read\n    crm.schemas.deals.write\n    crm.objects.owners.read\n    settings.users.read\n    crm.objects.users.read\n    ```\n\n6. Click **Create app** and copy your Access Token. Store it securely.\n\nFor more details, see the <a href=\"https://developers.hubspot.com/beta-docs/guides/apps/private-apps/overview\" target=\"_blank\" rel=\"noopener\"><span>HubSpot Private App documentation</span></a>\n\n\n"
