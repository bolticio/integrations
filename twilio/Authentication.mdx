# Twilio

With Twilio modules in Boltic, you can manage the messages and calls in your Twilio account.
To use the Twilio modules, you must have a Twilio account. You can create an account at [Twilio](https://www.twilio.com/login/).

## Authentication Ways

- Auth Token

### Auth Token

- Obtain your SID and Auth Token in Twilio
    - Log in to your Twilio account.

    - Go to Dashboard > the Develop tab. In the Account info section, copy your Account SID and Auth Token.
    
    - Connect Twilio with Boltic - Add SID and Auth Token > Save & Test.
