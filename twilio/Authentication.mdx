"# Twilio\n\nWith Twilio modules in Boltic, you can manage the messages and calls in your Twilio account.\nTo use the Twilio modules, you must have a Twilio account. You can create an account at [Twilio](https://www.twilio.com/login/).\n\n## Authentication Ways\n\n- Auth Token\n\n### Auth Token\n\n- Obtain your SID and Auth Token in Twilio\n    - Log in to your Twilio account.\n\n    - Go to Dashboard > the Develop tab. In the Account info section, copy your Account SID and Auth Token.\n    \n    - Connect Twilio with Boltic - Add SID and Auth Token > Save & Test.\n\n"
